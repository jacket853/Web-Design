A Niche Marketer to a Global Brand
Hennessey Performance Engineering was founded by John Hennessey in 1991 in Sealy, Texas. After winning several races and setting world speed records for his class of cars, John Hennessey began modifying cars out of his garage. The fine-tuned high-horsepower cars became nationally recognized in various car magazines, and even the show Top Gear. Hennessey identifies his company as an "indepedent automotive tuner," but in 2017, turned to creating faster cars from scratch. The resulting "Hennesey Special Vehicles" began producing its own cars, such as the Venom GT, F5 Roadster and Coupe, and VelociRaptor SUV.
